# Partnership Calls — Allied Healthcare Platforms
**Target segment:** Diagnostic labs, telemedicine platforms, pharmacy chains, specialist referral networks
**Goal:** Schedule and execute 2–3 discovery/exploration calls this week
**Time per call:** 30 minutes

---

## 1. Pre-call checklist

- [ ] Confirm 15-second intro of caller and org
- [ ] Check if contact has used or heard of Newtons AI
- [ ] Set agenda in first 30 seconds
- [ ] Record summary notes within 30 minutes post-call

---

## 2. 30-minute call agenda

| Time | Section | Owner |
|------|---------|-------|
| 0:00–1:00 | Rapport + context | Caller |
| 1:00–4:00 | Their world: pain points and workflows | Interviewee |
| 4:00–12:00 | Our world: Newtons AI platform value props mapped to their pain | Caller |
| 12:00–22:00 | Co-creation: what a lightweight partnership could look like | Both |
| 22:00–27:00 | Next steps: scope, timeline, and decision-maker mapping | Both |
| 27:00–30:00 | Clear close + follow-up commitment | Caller |

---

## 3. Opening script (first minute)

> "Thanks for the time. I'm calling from Newtons AI. We help doctors and clinics run their practice more efficiently. I have 30 minutes and want to focus on how we might collaborate — whether that's connecting your users with our doctor network, integrating data flows, or combining our patient-engagement tools. Does that sound useful?"

---

## 4. Core talking points

### A. Their pain (probe with questions)
- "When a patient finishes a consultation with you, what happens next in their care journey?"
- "Do you have to manually send reports or prescriptions to other providers?"
- "What's the gap between patient intent and actual care completion?"

### B. Our value
- Newtons AI connects doctors, patients, and allied providers on one data layer
- Patients retain portable health records across providers
- Shared consent layer meets regulatory requirements
- Outcome tracking across the care pathway

### C. Partnership models (offer 3, ask preference)
1. **Referral loop** — they refer patients to our network; we share structured handoff data
2. **Integration bridge** — one-way or two-way data sync with consent tokens
3. **Co-branded care pathway** — jointly designed workflow for a condition (e.g., diabetes, cardiac rehab)

### D. Proof and credibility
- "Currently live with X clinics; Yk+ patient records on platform"
- "Regulatory-compliant consent and audit trails built in"
- "Average 20% reduction in follow-up drop-off for connected providers"

### E. Next-step asks
- "Who else on your side should be in the next conversation?"
- "What's the one outcome that would make this partnership a clear win in 90 days?"
- "Can we schedule a 30-minute follow-up with that person by end of week?"

---

## 5. Closing template

> "This was useful. To recap: we'll explore [specific model] with [their contact], and I'll send a one-page summary by [date]. If we hit [their stated win condition] by [date], we commit to a pilot starting [date]. Does that feel fair?"
