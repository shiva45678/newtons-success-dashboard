# Follow-Up Templates

## Template 1 — Post-Call Summary (Partnership)

**Use:** Send within 30 minutes of a partnership discovery call
**Channel:** Email
**Subject:** Quick recap — Newtons AI x [Company] partnership call

---

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for the conversation today. Here's a quick recap:

- [Three bullet points: their top pain, our relevant capability, proposed model]
- Next step: [specific ask, e.g., "Share the current referral workflow with our integrations lead by Thursday"]
- Follow-up call: [proposed date/time] with [names]

I'll send a one-page summary by tomorrow morning. Let me know if anything above needs adjusting.

Best,
[Your name]
[Title]
[Phone]
[Website]

---

## Template 2 — Post-Call Summary (HMS Upgrade Lead)

**Use:** Send after a doctor expresses interest in the 1-week HMS upgrade
**Channel:** WhatsApp / SMS + email
**Subject:** HMS Upgrade — next steps

---

Hi Dr. [Last Name],

Great speaking with you today. As discussed, here are the next steps for your 1-week Easy HMS Upgrade:

1. Schedule your kickoff call: [calendar link]
2. Confirm patient data backup window: [two options]
3. Join the onboarding group: [invite link]

Your success manager for this week is [name] — reachable at [phone].

Questions? Reply here or call [support line].

Best,
Newtons AI Team

---

## Template 3 — Gentle Reminder (No Response in 48h)

**Use:** Partner who hasn't responded to the first follow-up
**Channel:** WhatsApp / SMS
**Copy:**

> Hi [First Name], circling back on the partnership idea we discussed. If the timing isn't right, no pressure — just let me know and I'll check in in a couple of weeks.

## Template 4 — Deadline Reminder (HMS Upgrade)

**Use:** Send Saturday morning to doctors who claimed a slot but haven't scheduled kickoff
**Channel:** SMS + email
**Copy:**

> Reminder: Your 1-week HMS Upgrade slot for this cohort ends Sunday. If you want to keep it, please confirm your kickoff time by tonight. [Link]

---

## Template 5 — Post-Pilot / Go-Live Check-in

**Use:** Day after the HMS upgrade go-live
**Channel:** WhatsApp + email
**Copy:**

> Thanks for completing the upgrade yesterday. Quick question: did everything go smoothly? Reply with a 1–5 rating and one thing we can improve. We'll follow up with a detailed survey next week.
